### **Current POI Database:**

**Supabase (PostgreSQL):**
- **200,000+ POIs** stored in structured format
- Includes: Google Places data, manual entries, enriched metadata
- Tables: `pois`, `destinations`, `poi_metadata`, `poi_reviews`
- Storage: Full details including descriptions, ratings, coordinates, photos

**Neo4j (Knowledge Graph):**
- **10,000+ verified POIs** with relationship mappings
- Focus on luxury POIs with high confidence scores (80%+)
- **100,000+ relationships** between POIs, themes, destinations, activities
- Optimized for complex queries and experience design

**Why the Difference?**
- Supabase holds ALL discovered POIs (raw data from Google Places, scraping, uploads)
- Neo4j holds CURATED luxury POIs with verified relationships
- Neo4j = Quality-focused subset for AI-powered experience design
- Captain network continuously verifies and promotes POIs from Supabase ‚Üí Neo4j
